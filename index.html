Your Ultimate Music Experience

UTOPIA is a desktop music player that allows you to stream and manage your favorite music from YouTube. With a clean, intuitive interface, you can search, play, and organize your music collection seamlessly.

Why Google OAuth Access is Needed

To provide you with the best experience, UTOPIA requires access to your Google account to:

  • Authenticate your identity securely
  • Access YouTube's music content through official APIs
  • Manage your playlists and preferences

We only request the minimum permissions necessary for functionality and never access or store your password.

Data Usage Transparency

We are committed to protecting your privacy:

  • We only access data necessary for app functionality
  • Your data is never sold to third parties
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google account settings
  • All data is securely stored and processed
