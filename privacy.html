Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 14, 2025

Information We Collect

When you use UTOPIA with Google OAuth, we collect:

  • Your Google user ID (for authentication purposes only)
  • Basic profile information (name, email) as provided by Google
  • YouTube playlist data (only when you choose to access it)
  • Usage data to improve our service

We do not collect or store passwords, payment information, or any other sensitive personal data.

How We Use OAuth Data

The information we collect through Google OAuth is used solely for:

  • Authenticating your identity to provide secure access
  • Accessing YouTube content as requested by you
  • Personalizing your experience within the app
  • Improving our service based on usage patterns

Data Protection Commitment

We are committed to protecting your data:

  • We do not sell, rent, or misuse your personal information
  • We do not share your data with third parties except as required for service functionality
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • We retain data only as long as necessary for service provision

Data Storage, Deletion, and User Rights

Your data is stored securely on our servers. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke app permissions through your Google account settings at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

GDPR and CCPA Compliance

We comply with data protection laws including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). This includes:

  • Lawful, fair, and transparent processing of personal data
  • Purpose limitation - we only use data for specified purposes
  • Data minimization - we only collect necessary information
  • Accuracy - we ensure personal data is accurate
  • Storage limitation - we retain data only as long as necessary
  • Integrity and confidentiality - we implement appropriate security measures
